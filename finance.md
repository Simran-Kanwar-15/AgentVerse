F&O Key Concepts for Finance Agent Chatbot
Call Option (CE): Right to BUY the underlying asset at strike price before expiry
Put Option (PE): Right to SELL the underlying asset at strike price before expiry
Strike Price: Pre-agreed price at which the option can be exercised
Expiry: Date on which the F&O contract expires (weekly/monthly on Groww)
Premium: Price paid to buy an option contract
Open Interest (OI): Total outstanding contracts not yet settled
Implied Volatility (IV): Market's expectation of future price movement
Greeks: Delta, Gamma, Theta, Vega — measure sensitivity of option price to various factors
Lot Size: Minimum quantity of contracts in one F&O trade (varies by underlying)
Scalper Mode: Groww's rapid one-tap execution mode for F&O traders on mobile
