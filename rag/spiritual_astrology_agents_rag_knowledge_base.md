# What is Astrology

Astrology is the study of celestial bodies and their influence on human personality, emotions, behavior, and life events. Vedic astrology uses planetary positions at the time of birth to generate a Kundli or birth chart.

Astrology includes:
- zodiac signs
- planets
- houses
- nakshatras
- planetary transits
- doshas
- dashas
- compatibility analysis
- spiritual timing systems
